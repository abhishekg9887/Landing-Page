About the BTech Program

Our Bachelor of Technology (BTech) program offers a comprehensive curriculum designed to provide students with a solid foundation in engineering and technology. With experienced faculty, state-of-the-art facilities, and a focus on practical experience, this program prepares students for a successful career in various technology fields.

  • Duration: 4 Years
  • Specializations: Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Civil Engineering
  • Admission Criteria: High School Diploma with a strong emphasis on mathematics and science
  • Career Opportunities: Software Developer, Data Analyst, Mechanical Engineer, Civil Engineer, and more
